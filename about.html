Frequently asked questions

What is the lead time for new orders?

Most "In Stock" vehicles are dispatched within 14 days, while custom builds range from 3 to 6 months depending on specification.

How does pricing work?

We operate on a transparency model. The price you see is the price. No hidden fees. No middleman markups.

What happens at the end of the lease?

Simply hand back the keys or upgrade to the latest model. Our inspectors use industry-standard fair wear and tear guidelines.

Is insurance included?

Standard leasing covers the vehicle, road tax, and delivery. Insurance is typically the driver's responsibility.
