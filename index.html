We operate on a zero-margin transparency model. The price you see is the price we've algorithmically secured from our direct global supply chain. No hidden fees. No middleman markups.

Our "Silk Smooth" return process means you simply hand back the keys or upgrade to the latest model. Our inspectors use industry-standard fair wear and tear guidelines to ensure absolute fairness.
